# Brand Guidelines — GreenScape Landscaping

## Brand Overview
- Brand Name: GreenScape
- Industry: Landscaping & Garden Design
- Tagline: "Where Nature Meets Design"
- Target Audience: Homeowners (35-65), property developers, commercial estates in the UK
- Brand Tone: Bold & confident, earthy & grounded, premium but approachable

## Color Palette
- Primary: #1B5E20 (Deep Forest Green)
- Secondary: #2E7D32 (Emerald Green)
- Accent: #C8E6C9 (Soft Mint)
- Warm: #5D4037 (Rich Earth Brown)
- Background (Dark): #0C1F0E (Deep Green-Black)
- Background (Light): #F5F5F0 (Warm Cream)
- Text (Primary): #1A1A1A
- Text (Secondary): #6B6B6B
- Highlight: #FFB300 (Golden Amber — for CTAs and accents)

## Typography
- Heading Font: Playfair Display (serif — premium, established feel)
- Body Font: DM Sans (clean, modern, highly readable)
- Heading Weight: 700, 800
- Body Weight: 400, 500

## Imagery Style
- Photo style: Bright, natural light, golden hour shots of gardens and landscapes
- Preferred types: Before/after transformations, aerial drone shots, close-up plant details, finished garden reveals
- Mood: Lush, alive, aspirational, premium craftsmanship

## Services
1. Garden Design — Custom garden layouts, planting schemes, 3D visualisations
2. Landscaping — Complete garden builds, groundwork, drainage, turf
3. Paving & Patios — Natural stone, porcelain, block paving, bespoke patterns
4. Tree Surgery — Crown reduction, felling, stump grinding, hedge trimming
5. Lawn Care — Seeding, turfing, aeration, seasonal treatment plans
6. Maintenance — Ongoing garden care, seasonal clean-ups, commercial contracts

## Design Preferences
- Bold hero with parallax scroll
- Before/after portfolio section (the money-maker for landscapers)
- Stats counters (years experience, gardens completed, 5-star reviews)
- Earthy, organic feel but with premium polish
- Scroll-triggered animations throughout
- Video-ready hero section
